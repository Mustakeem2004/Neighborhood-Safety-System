The Protocol.
Three steps. Zero delay.

Built for speed and signal — not noise. Every alert is filtered, verified, and dispatched before it reaches your screen.

01

Report

Trigger an alert in seconds via mobile or web. Photos, location and category — all optional, all anonymous.

02

Verify

Reports cross-reference with neighboring nodes. Multi-source confirmation flags credibility before broadcast.

03

Broadcast

Verified alerts push instantly to every member inside a 5km radius — and to local dispatch when severity demands.
